Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the program?

The program is a total of 13 weeks of content and instruction plus 1 week to study for and take the final exam.

Is the program fully online?

Yes! The entire Health & Wellness Coaching Program is 100% online, so you can enroll regardless of where you live.

Do I need prior health and wellness experience?

No prior experience is required! Whether you have a background in health and wellness or not, you can thrive in this program. We teach the fundamentals of health and wellness coaching, which you can use to get started in coaching or enhance your existing practice.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes! A certificate of completion will be provided upon successfully graduating from the program. This certificate will qualify you to sit for the NBHWC exam which, upon passing, would give you the designation of Nationally Board Certified Health and Wellness Coach (NBC-HWC).

Is this program NBHWC approved?

Yes! Our program is officially board-approved and will fulfill the requirement of completing a board-approved training program to sit for the NBHWC exam.
